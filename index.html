Albert Einstein

One of the greatest physicists of all time

  • 1879Albert Einstein was born in Ulm, in the Kingdom of Wurttemberg in the German Empire.
  • 1896He enrolled in the four-year mathematics and physics teaching diploma program at the Federal Polytechnic School.
  • 1901He acquired Swiss citizenship and got a job in Bern at the Swiss Patent Office.
  • 1905He completed his dissertation A New Determination of Molecular Dimensions and published four groundbreaking papers on the photoelectric effect, Brownian motion, special relativity, and the equivalence of mass and energy.
  • 1906He was awarded a PhD.
  • 1908He was appointed lecturer at the University of Bern.
  • 1911He became a professor at the German Charles-Ferdinand University.
  • 1912He was a professor of theoretical physics at the ETH Zurich, where he taught analytical mechanics and thermodynamics.
  • 1913He became the Prussian Academy of Sciences in Berlin.
  • 1915He completed his general theory of relativity.
  • 1922He was awarded the 1921 Nobel Prize in Physics.
  • 1955He died at the age of 76.
